### Mission

Bridge is a digital collaboration platform that standardises the flow of orders, delivery data, and transport documents
across Norway's grocery and hospitality sectors. From day one I focused on both the shared front-end foundation and the
Bridge Transport module—a modern transport management system (TMS) for carriers and wholesalers.

### Highlights

- **Front end** – Designed the component architecture, styling system, and transport workflows using **React**, **SCSS**,
  **React Hook Form**, **TanStack Query**, and **TanStack Table**.
- **Back end** – Built OpenAPI-first services in **Kotlin**/**Spring Boot**, integrating with adjacent systems and
  messaging pipelines.
- **CI/CD** – Automated builds, linting, testing, container packaging, and deployments to **Azure Kubernetes Service**
  via **Azure Pipelines**.
- **Testing** – Added comprehensive unit and integration coverage with **Jest**, **JUnit**, **MockK**, **Mongock**, and
  **TestContainers**.
- **Observability** – Wired up **Datadog** dashboards and tracing to give product teams real-time insight into transport
  execution.

### Impact

Bridge replaced manual, paper-heavy processes with a shared digital infrastructure. The transport module now delivers
real-time routing, automated communications, scanning-based tracking, and live ETA information—driving fewer deviations
and better customer service. The architecture and tooling I helped establish underpin ongoing feature work and new
integrations.
