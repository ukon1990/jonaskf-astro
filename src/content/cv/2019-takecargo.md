### Mission

TakeCargo connects transport buyers, suppliers, and carriers through a shared digital workflow. Our goal was to remove
paper-based processes, standardise data exchange (GS1/STAND), and improve traceability throughout the transport chain.

### Highlights

- Worked as a **full-stack developer** in a three-person team running **Scrum** with three-week sprints.
- Rebuilt the **React** front end, migrated key views to **TypeScript**, introduced **Redux** patterns, and tightened
  coverage with **Jest** tests.
- Extended and maintained **Dropwizard** and **Spring Boot** microservices running in **Azure Kubernetes Service**.
- Delivered customer reporting with a **serverless** pipeline powered by **Azure Logic Apps** and scheduled functions.
- Managed CI/CD with **Azure Pipelines**, containerising services and coordinating deployments to shared environments.
- Applied **Bootstrap** and **SCSS** to keep the UI responsive and consistent across the portal.

### Impact

The revamped platform automated manual handoffs, removed paper workflows, and gave the business higher quality data for
transport planning. The added test suite and deployment automation reduced lead time while keeping releases dependable.
